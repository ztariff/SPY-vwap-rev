Key insight: Credit ratio is the single most powerful filter. Going from no filter (CR ≥ 0%) to CR ≥ 15% eliminates 170 losses (78%) while only cutting trades from 1,511 → 575, and more than doubles the Sharpe ratio. Adding the 0.40/0.30δ exclusion further eliminates 41 more losses at the cost of trade volume, leaving only 7 losses across the entire 4+ year backtest. The tradeoff is always fewer trades — but the remaining trades are dramatically higher quality.
