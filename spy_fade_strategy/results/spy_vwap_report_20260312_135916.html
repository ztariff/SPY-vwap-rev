Methodology

  • VWAP: Session VWAP reset at 9:30 ET, cumsum(TP×V)/cumsum(V) on 1-min bars.
  • ATR: 14-period Wilder's smoothed ATR (prior day's value used for signal day).
  • Entry: First bar where |close − VWAP| ≥ ATR × multiplier.
  • ATR Grid: 0.5x through 2.0x in 0.1 steps (16 levels).
  • Scale-In: Enter partial at level A, add at level B; 50/50 allocation; exits on averaged cost basis.
  • Options: ALL P&L from real Polygon market data. Zero Black-Scholes.
  • Delta Selection: Strike distance proxy only for selecting which contracts to pull. P&L is always real prices.
  • Both Directions: Above VWAP = fade (short), Below VWAP = buy (long). Identical framework, mirrored.
